Hobbies & Interests

Health & Fitness

I first became interested in health & fitness during my time at university where I started to use and learn about the gym.

After helping my friends to get started and get results I loved that feeling so much that I became a personal trainer.

Since then I've trained in and explored various different kinds of exercise from calisthenics to skateboarding to parkour.

Music & Performing

I've played guitar since I was 8 years old, started to teach myself piano at 25 and I've sung for as long as I can remember.

When I was 27 I joined a local theatre group, performed in musicals and the following year performed as Robin Hood at the Epstein Theatre in a musical based in Sherwood Forest.

I've done verious shows since and currently sing in care homes for the elderly and at parties and weddings with my band Square Park.
